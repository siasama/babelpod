BabelPod 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-04-22

1. 我们收集什么信息

BabelPod 默认不收集可识别个人身份的信息。

2. 数据处理方式

  • 你的音频、转写文本、笔记与设置主要保存在你的设备本地。
  • 我们不会将你的个人内容上传到我们的服务器进行分析或售卖。
  • 你通过系统“分享”功能导出的内容，由你主动选择的目标应用处理。

3. 第三方服务与跟踪

当前版本不集成广告 SDK，不进行跨 App 跟踪。

4. 权限说明

如 App 请求系统权限（例如麦克风、文件访问等），仅用于实现对应功能，并在系统弹窗中说明用途。

5. 支持平台与系统版本

本应用支持 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac，当前最低系统版本为 iOS 18+ / macOS 15+。

6. 数据控制

你可通过删除 App 移除本地数据，也可在系统设置中管理权限授权。

7. 未成年人

本应用不以未成年人为目标用户，也不会有意收集未成年人个人信息。

8. 政策更新

我们可能更新本政策，更新后会在本页面发布最新版本。

9. 联系方式

BabelPod Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-22

1. What We Collect

BabelPod does not collect personally identifiable information by default.

2. How Data Is Processed

  • Your audio, transcripts, notes, and settings are primarily stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload your personal content to our servers for analytics or sale.
  • Content exported via the system share sheet is processed by the destination app you choose.

3. Third-Party Services and Tracking

The current version includes no ad SDK and does not perform cross-app tracking.

4. Permissions

If the app requests system permissions (for example, microphone or file access), they are used only to provide related features and are explained in iOS permission prompts.

5. Supported Platforms and Minimum OS Versions

This app supports iPhone, iPad, and Mac. The current minimum OS versions are iOS 18+ and macOS 15+.

6. Your Control

You can remove local data by deleting the app. You can also manage permissions in system settings.

7. Children

This app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: 1124549218@qq.com
